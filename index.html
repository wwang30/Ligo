Two Children Test:
‍

In order to avoid the situation of parents doing decision for their children: Optimized the app interface for children's preferences, so that children can become the main users of the Ligo App and choose the game themselves. At the same time, highlight the benefits of the game to convince parents.

Ligo App

All Functions
at a glance

The main page generates characters based on the user's dream career. All the function entrances of the Ligo App are tiled on the main page, which is simple and intuitive: the lower-left corner directly leads to the Library and Game Center; Press the lower right corner icon to adjust and view the real-time status of LIGO.

Generate plans
for dreams

The app will generate a skill training plan for kids based on their chosen dream career. Meanwhile, the characters on the main page will also change with the user’s every level upgrade.

Algorithm recommendation
based on interest

After selecting several areas of interest, the users will receive game recommendations. Which helps them explore their own interests in the process of playing.

Find more fun in the
Game Center

In the game center, users can explore the endless possibilities of LIGO. With the growth of users' age, the game center is constantly updating to accompany users better.
